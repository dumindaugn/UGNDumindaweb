About Me

As the Senior Media Officer at the Parliament of Sri Lanka, I play a pivotal role in shaping public perception of legislative processes and ensuring transparent communication between Parliament and the public.

With extensive experience in political communication, I specialize in crisis management, media relations, and strategic communication, working to strengthen democratic processes through effective information dissemination.

Areas of Expertise

Media Relations

Managing relationships with national and international media outlets, organizing briefings, and ensuring accurate information dissemination.

Crisis Communication

Strategic management of information flow during politically sensitive situations and national crises.

Digital Strategy

Developing and implementing comprehensive social media strategies for parliamentary communication.
